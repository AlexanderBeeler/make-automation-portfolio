# Lead Management System

## Problem

Businesses often receive leads through online forms and must manually record the information, notify staff, and send confirmation emails. This process is repetitive and can lead to missed or delayed follow-ups.

---

## Solution

This automation captures new Google Form submissions, stores the lead information in Google Sheets, notifies the team through Discord, and automatically sends a confirmation email to the customer.

---

## Workflow

Google Forms → Make → Router

Branch 1:
Google Sheets (Store Lead Information)

Branch 2:
Discord (Notify Team)

Branch 3:
Gmail (Send Confirmation Email)
